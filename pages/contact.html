Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? Check our FAQs or reach out directly

We welcome partnerships with organizations across sectors. Contact our partnerships team at partnerships@nexgen.org to discuss collaboration opportunities. We offer a range of services including consulting, training, capacity building, and project support.

Our process typically involves: (1) Initial assessment and needs analysis, (2) Proposal development, (3) Engagement and implementation, (4) Regular monitoring and adjustment, and (5) Final evaluation and handover. We tailor each engagement to your specific needs and context.

Training programs vary based on content and objectives, ranging from 1-day workshops to 6-month intensive programs. We can customize the duration and format to meet your organization's needs and participant schedules.

While our primary focus is the UK and West Africa, we have experience working on international projects and can engage with organizations globally. Contact us to discuss your specific geographic requirements.

We welcome donations through multiple channels. Click the "Donate" button on our website to make a contribution. We are a registered charity, and donations are tax-deductible. For large donations or special funding requests, contact our partnerships team.

Our team consists of experienced professionals with advanced degrees, professional certifications, and extensive field experience. Team members have worked with leading international organizations and have deep expertise in their respective areas.
